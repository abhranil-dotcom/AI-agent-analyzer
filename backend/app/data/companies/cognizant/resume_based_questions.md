## How Cognizant Probes Your Resume

Cognizant interviewers typically use a resume project as a starting point before pivoting into
general technical questions, similar to other IT-services firms — but for vertical-specific roles
(healthcare, BFSI), they often add a domain-flavored twist, asking you to imagine how your project
or skills would apply to a regulated, data-sensitive context.

Common follow-up angles:
- "Walk me through this project — what was your specific role?"
- "How did you ensure correctness or handle errors in this project?"
- "If this system handled sensitive financial or health data, what would you need to change?"
- "What was the hardest bug or issue you had to fix?"

For candidates with modern backend or AI projects, expect at least one question testing genuine
understanding of the underlying concept, not just that a tool/library was used.
