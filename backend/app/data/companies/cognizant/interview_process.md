## Hiring Process

## For freshers (GenC)

1. **Online Assessment** — quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, verbal ability, and a coding
   section. Candidates scoring higher on the coding section may be tracked into **GenC Pro** or
   **GenC Elevate** (harder coding round, DSA-heavy, leading to more technically demanding
   roles).
2. **Technical Interview** — covers CS fundamentals, the primary language on the resume, and
   projects. GenC Pro/Elevate candidates face a meaningfully harder round with live coding and
   deeper DSA/system questions.
3. **HR Interview** — communication, relocation flexibility across Cognizant's delivery centers,
   and career motivation.

## For lateral/experienced hires

1. **Resume screening** against the specific role and vertical (healthcare, BFSI, etc.)
   requirements.
2. **1-2 Technical Interviews** — deep dive into the relevant stack and, for healthcare/BFSI
   roles, domain-specific knowledge (e.g. claims processing concepts, regulatory considerations
   like HIPAA).
3. **HR/Managerial round** — compensation, account/vertical fitment, and notice period.

## Timeline

Campus GenC hiring typically takes 4-8 weeks from assessment to offer, including result
processing for large batch drives. Lateral hiring is usually 2-4 weeks depending on role urgency
and vertical specialization required.

## What's assessed at each stage

The online assessment filters for aptitude and coding ability (with GenC Pro/Elevate as a harder
track); the technical round filters for CS fundamentals, stack depth, and — for vertical-specific
roles — domain awareness; the HR round covers logistics and cultural fit.
