## About Cognizant

Cognizant is a US-headquartered IT services and consulting company with a very large India-based
delivery workforce. It's especially strong in healthcare, life sciences, and BFSI (banking,
financial services, insurance) verticals, and has built a distinct identity around
industry-specific digital transformation rather than purely generalist IT services.

## Business lines relevant to tech hiring

- **Healthcare & life sciences IT**: a flagship practice area, including claims processing
  systems, health-data platforms, and regulatory-compliance-heavy applications.
- **BFSI digital engineering**: core banking modernization, payments, and fraud/risk platforms.
- **Cloud & digital engineering**: full-stack development (Java, .NET, React), cloud migration,
  and API/microservices work.
- **Data & AI**: data engineering, analytics, and applied AI solutions across its verticals.
- **IoT & engineering services**: manufacturing and retail-focused digital engineering.

## Culture and hiring style

Cognizant hires largely through its **GenC (Genesis of Cognizant)** program for freshers,
alongside GenC Pro/Elevate tracks for stronger technical candidates, plus lateral hiring for
experienced roles. Given its strong healthcare/BFSI focus, domain adaptability and comfort with
regulated, process-heavy environments are valued alongside core technical skill. The technical bar
for GenC is moderate; GenC Pro/Elevate and lateral roles are noticeably higher.

## Typical roles hired for

Programmer Analyst Trainee (GenC, freshers), Programmer Analyst (GenC Pro/Elevate, stronger
freshers), Full Stack Developer, Cloud Engineer, Data Engineer — plus senior lateral roles in
healthcare/BFSI domain consulting and solution architecture.
