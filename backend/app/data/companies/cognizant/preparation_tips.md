## Preparation Tips

- Practice the GenC assessment's aptitude + coding format; a strong coding score can move you into
  the tougher but more technically interesting GenC Pro/Elevate track.
- Revise OOP, SQL transactions/ACID properties, and basic exception handling — these come up
  regardless of vertical.
- If applying to a healthcare or BFSI-focused account, learn the basic concept of why data
  correctness and privacy matter in those domains (deep domain expertise isn't expected).
- Prepare to discuss comfort working within regulated, process-heavy environments.
- Be ready to confidently answer questions about relocating across Cognizant's delivery centers.
- For GenC Pro/Elevate candidates, revise DSA fundamentals (linked lists, HashMaps, binary search)
  since the round is noticeably harder than the general track.
