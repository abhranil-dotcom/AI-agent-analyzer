## HR Questions

### Tell me about yourself.
Keep it structured and clearly articulated — Capgemini weighs communication clarity heavily given
its international client base, so pace and clarity matter as much as content.

### Why do you want to join Capgemini?
Reference its global, client-facing nature (clients across Europe and the US), the breadth of
engineering and consulting work, and specific practice areas that interest you (e.g. SAP, cloud).

### How comfortable are you communicating with international clients or teams?
Answer honestly and give a concrete example if you have one — cross-cultural or remote
collaboration experience (even from group projects or internships) is a plus here.

### Are you willing to relocate or work in rotational shifts to support global clients?
Some Capgemini accounts require overlap with European or US time zones — be upfront about your
flexibility.

### Describe a time you had to explain something technical to a non-technical person.
Directly relevant to Capgemini's consulting-heavy work — use a specific, real example.

### What are your strengths and weaknesses?
Ground strengths in evidence; for weaknesses, choose something genuine with a clear improvement
plan.

### Describe a challenge you faced working in a team and how you resolved it.
Structure as situation, action, outcome — emphasize collaboration and communication.

### Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
Frame around deepening expertise in a specific practice area (e.g. cloud, SAP) within Capgemini.

### Why should we hire you?
Tie specific skills to the role and mention adaptability across technologies/domains, which
Capgemini values given its diverse client portfolio.

### How do you handle ambiguous or changing requirements?
Consulting-style work often involves shifting client requirements — describe a real instance of
adapting to changing scope.

### Do you have any questions for us?
Ask something specific — e.g. which practice area or account you might be placed in, or what the
onboarding/training path looks like.

### What do you know about Capgemini's global presence?
Mention it's headquartered in France with major delivery operations in India, serving clients
across automotive, financial services, telecom, and manufacturing worldwide.
