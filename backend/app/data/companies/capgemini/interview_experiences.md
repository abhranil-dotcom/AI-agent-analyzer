## Candidate Experiences

### Candidate A — Analyst (Campus, general track)
"The assessment had the usual aptitude sections plus a coding round with two Easy-Medium
problems. What stood out was a separate English communication assessment — a short recorded
speaking test. The technical interview covered OOP basics, a SQL join question, and questions
about my resume project. The interviewer specifically asked how comfortable I'd be working with
teams across different countries, which caught me off guard a little, but I answered honestly
about my group project experience with international students."

### Candidate B — SAP Technical Consultant (Campus, SAP track)
"I applied specifically for the SAP track. The technical round mixed general CS fundamentals with
SAP-specific questions — what's the difference between functional and technical consulting, and a
basic scenario about a business process. I didn't have prior SAP experience, and that was fine —
they were mainly checking if I understood the concept of business processes and had strong
programming fundamentals to build on with training."

### Candidate C — Cloud Engineer (Lateral, 2 years experience)
"Two technical rounds: the first covered core cloud concepts (IaC, containerization, load
balancing) and a Terraform-based scenario question. The second was more architecture-focused —
they gave me a case study about migrating a monolith to microservices and asked me to talk
through my approach. Communication clarity clearly mattered a lot; the interviewer commented
positively on how I structured my answers. HR discussed compensation and account placement."

### Candidate D — Digital Full Stack Developer (Lateral)
"The first technical round was mostly Java/Spring Boot and a live coding exercise (queue using
two stacks). The second round dug into a REST API I'd built previously — they asked about
pagination, error handling, and versioning strategy. Overall the interviews felt collaborative,
almost like a design discussion rather than a strict test. HR was efficient — offer came about 10
days after the final round."
