## Preparation Tips

- Practice explaining technical concepts in clear, simple language — Capgemini explicitly
  assesses communication given its international, client-facing work.
- If there's a spoken-English/communication assessment in your drive, treat it seriously; it's a
  real filter, not a formality.
- Revise core fundamentals (OOP, SQL joins, ACID properties) alongside your primary stack.
- If applying to the SAP track, learn the basic distinction between functional and technical
  consulting even without direct SAP experience.
- Prepare an example of explaining something technical to a non-technical audience — this comes
  up directly in HR rounds.
- Be ready to discuss comfort with rotational shifts or cross-timezone collaboration if relevant
  to the account you're applying for.
