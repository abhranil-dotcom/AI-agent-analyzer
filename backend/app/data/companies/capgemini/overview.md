## About Capgemini

Capgemini is a French-origin global leader in consulting, digital transformation, and technology
services, with a large delivery presence in India. It works across industries — automotive,
financial services, telecom, and manufacturing — helping clients modernize applications, migrate
to the cloud, and adopt data/AI at scale.

## Business lines relevant to tech hiring

- **Cloud & infrastructure**: AWS, Azure, and GCP migration and managed services.
- **Digital engineering**: full-stack development (Java, .NET, React, Angular), mobile, and
  API/microservices architecture.
- **Enterprise platforms**: SAP (a particularly strong practice area for Capgemini globally) and
  Salesforce implementation.
- **Data & AI**: data engineering, analytics, and applied AI/ML solutions.
- **Engineering & R&D services**: automotive and manufacturing-focused embedded/software
  engineering (a Capgemini specialty relative to pure IT-services peers).

## Culture and hiring style

Capgemini hires through campus drives, off-campus pooled drives, and lateral hiring, with a
noticeably international/European client-facing culture even for India-based roles — clients are
often in France, Germany, the UK, and the US. Communication skills and client-facing polish carry
somewhat more weight here than at some peers. The technical bar is moderate for freshers and
scales up meaningfully for lateral SAP/cloud/architecture roles.

## Typical roles hired for

Analyst / Senior Analyst (freshers), Software Engineer, SAP Consultant (functional and
technical), Cloud Engineer, and Automotive/Embedded Software Engineer — plus senior lateral roles
in solution architecture and engagement management.
