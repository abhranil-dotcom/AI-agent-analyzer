## Hiring Process

## For freshers

1. **Online Assessment** — quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, verbal ability, and a coding
   section (typically 1-2 problems, Easy-to-Medium, plus programming MCQs).
2. **Technical Interview** — covers CS fundamentals, the candidate's primary programming
   language, and academic/personal projects. For candidates applying to specific tracks (SAP,
   cloud), expect track-specific questions even at the fresher level.
3. **HR / Versant (English communication) round** — many Capgemini drives include an automated or
   human-assessed English communication check, reflecting the client-facing nature of much of its
   work, followed by a standard HR discussion on relocation and career motivation.

## For lateral/experienced hires

1. **Resume screening** against the specific role and account requirements.
2. **1-2 Technical Interviews** — deep dive into the relevant stack (e.g. SAP module,
   Java/microservices, cloud platform), architecture decisions, and past project ownership.
3. **HR/Managerial round** — compensation, account/project fitment, and notice period.

## Timeline

Campus hiring typically takes 4-6 weeks from assessment to offer. Lateral hiring varies more
widely by account urgency, from 2 weeks to over a month for specialized SAP or architecture
roles.

## What's assessed at each stage

The online assessment filters for aptitude and coding literacy; the technical round filters for
domain/stack depth; the communication check specifically filters for client-facing readiness,
which Capgemini weighs more heavily than some IT-services peers given its international client
base; the HR round covers logistics and cultural fit.
