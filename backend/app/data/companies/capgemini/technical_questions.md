### Explain encapsulation and abstraction with examples.
Expect a real-world analogy for each and a clear distinction between the two.

### What is the difference between a shallow copy and a deep copy?
Cover reference copying vs full duplication of nested objects, with a language-specific example.

### Explain ACID properties in database transactions.
Cover Atomicity, Consistency, Isolation, Durability with a one-line explanation each.

### What is the difference between synchronous and asynchronous programming?
Cover blocking vs non-blocking execution and a practical example (API calls, file I/O).
