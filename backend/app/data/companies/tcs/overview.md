## About TCS

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is India's largest IT services, consulting, and business
solutions company, part of the Tata Group. It operates across more than 55 countries and serves
clients in banking, insurance, retail, healthcare, manufacturing, and telecom through a mix of
application development, enterprise application services, cloud migration, and digital
engineering offerings.

## Business lines relevant to tech hiring

- **Digital & cloud engineering**: full-stack web development (Java, .NET, React, Angular),
  cloud migration on AWS/Azure/GCP, and DevOps.
- **Enterprise applications**: SAP, Salesforce, and Oracle implementation and support.
- **Legacy & mainframe**: COBOL, JCL, and mainframe modernization for large banking clients.
- **Data & AI**: data engineering, BI/analytics, and a growing AI/GenAI practice (TCS AI Cloud).
- **BPS & testing**: business process services and QA/test automation.

## Culture and hiring style

TCS hires overwhelmingly through mass campus recruitment (TCS NQT — National Qualifier Test)
alongside a smaller lateral/experienced-hire pipeline. Culture emphasizes discipline,
process-orientation (CMMI-driven delivery), long training pipelines (TCS iON, Ninja/Digital
Ignite programs), and internal mobility across projects and technologies. The bar for entry-level
technical depth is moderate — TCS invests heavily in post-hire training — but strong weight is
placed on aptitude, communication, and willingness to relocate/rotate across projects.

## Typical roles hired for

Assistant System Engineer / System Engineer (freshers), Digital/Full-Stack Developer, Cloud
Engineer, SAP Consultant, and Business Analyst — plus senior lateral roles in architecture and
delivery management.
