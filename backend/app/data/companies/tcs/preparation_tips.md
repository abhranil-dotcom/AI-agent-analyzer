## Preparation Tips

- Practice TCS NQT-style aptitude sections (quantitative, logical reasoning, verbal) alongside
  coding — the aptitude score is a hard filter for campus candidates.
- Revise core CS fundamentals (OOP, DBMS normalization, OS basics, networking) — TCS interviewers
  test breadth over deep specialization for entry-level roles.
- Be ready to explain your resume projects clearly and simply; TCS values communication clarity
  as much as technical correctness.
- If applying for a specific track (cloud, SAP, mainframe), skim the basics of that domain even
  without direct experience — interviewers assess trainability, not existing expertise.
- Prepare a clear, honest answer about relocation flexibility — this is asked in nearly every TCS
  HR round.
- Keep coding answers simple and correct rather than over-engineered; TCS's coding bar rewards
  clean, working solutions over premature optimization.
