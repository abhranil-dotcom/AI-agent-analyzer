## About Infosys

Infosys is one of India's largest IT services and consulting companies, headquartered in
Bengaluru with a globally recognized training campus in Mysore. It provides application
development, digital transformation, cloud, and consulting services to clients across banking,
insurance, retail, and manufacturing, and has built a strong internal brand around technical
upskilling.

## Business lines relevant to tech hiring

- **Digital & full-stack engineering**: Java, .NET, React, Angular, and mobile app development.
- **Cloud & DevOps**: migration and managed services on AWS, Azure, and GCP.
- **Enterprise platforms**: SAP, Oracle, and Salesforce implementation.
- **Data, analytics & AI**: data engineering, BI, and Infosys Topaz (its AI-first offerings suite).
- **Infosys BPM**: business process management services.

## Culture and hiring style

Infosys hires primarily through campus placement drives and its own online assessment, followed
by structured onboarding through Infosys Mysore — widely regarded as one of the largest corporate
training campuses in the world, running months-long foundation training for freshers. Infosys
also runs **InfyTQ**, a free learning and certification platform used both to build a talent
pipeline and to let candidates demonstrate skills ahead of interviews. Culture emphasizes
continuous learning, process discipline, and a structured career-progression model.

## Typical roles hired for

Systems Engineer / Digital Specialist Engineer (freshers, based on assessment tier), Power
Programmer (top-tier freshers with strong coding scores), Technology Analyst, Cloud Engineer, and
senior lateral roles in architecture, delivery, and domain consulting.
