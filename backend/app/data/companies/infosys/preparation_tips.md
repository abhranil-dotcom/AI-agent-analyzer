## Preparation Tips

- Practice the online assessment's pseudocode/output-prediction style questions, not just
  free-form coding — Infosys's format differs from typical LeetCode-style problems.
- If you score well on the coding section, be ready for the tougher Power Programmer round —
  brush up on DSA (graphs, DP) just in case.
- Complete a few InfyTQ modules or certifications beforehand; genuine familiarity with the
  platform helps in HR conversations about it.
- Revise OOP, DBMS joins/indexing, and basic networking — these come up regardless of track.
- Prepare to discuss your resume projects with a focus on what you learned, since Infosys values
  continuous-learning framing.
- Be ready to answer confidently about relocating to Mysore for initial training.
