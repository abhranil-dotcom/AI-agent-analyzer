## HR Questions

### Tell me about yourself.
Keep it structured: education, key project/internship, and a closing line connecting your
interests to the kind of work Infosys does — avoid simply restating your resume.

### Why do you want to join Infosys?
Reference Infosys's investment in training (Mysore campus, InfyTQ), the variety of client domains
you'd be exposed to, and structured career growth — avoid vague "great brand" answers.

### Are you willing to relocate for training and project placement?
Infosys freshers typically go through training at Mysore before project allocation, which may be
in a different city — answer honestly and confidently if you're flexible.

### What do you know about InfyTQ?
Mention it's Infosys's free learning and certification platform used to build technical skills
and, for some candidates, to strengthen their profile before or during the hiring process.

### Describe a time you had to learn a new technology quickly.
Use a specific example — a course, a project, or a hackathon — and describe how you approached
the learning process, not just the outcome.

### What are your strengths and weaknesses?
Ground your strength in a real example; for weaknesses, pick something genuine and describe a
concrete improvement action, not a disguised strength like "I work too hard."

### How do you handle deadline pressure?
Infosys delivery work is often deadline-driven — describe a real instance of prioritization or
time management under pressure.

### Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
Frame around deepening technical or domain expertise within Infosys and taking on more
responsibility, rather than an unrelated career pivot.

### Why should we hire you over other candidates?
Tie 2-3 concrete skills or experiences to what the role needs — specificity beats generic
self-praise.

### How do you approach working with a team on a shared codebase?
Emphasize communication, code review discipline, and version control practices if you have
relevant experience (even from academic group projects).

### Do you have any questions for us?
Ask something specific — e.g. about the training program structure, or how project allocation
works after Mysore — silence here can read as lack of genuine interest.

### How comfortable are you with continuous learning and certifications?
Infosys culture leans heavily on upskilling — mention any certifications you've completed or are
pursuing, even outside InfyTQ.
