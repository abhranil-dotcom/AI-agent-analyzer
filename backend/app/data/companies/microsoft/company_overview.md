## About Microsoft

Microsoft is a global technology leader spanning cloud computing (Azure), productivity software
(Microsoft 365, Teams), operating systems (Windows), developer tools (GitHub, Visual Studio,
TypeScript), and gaming (Xbox). Its engineering culture has shifted heavily over the past decade
toward a **"growth mindset"** philosophy — valuing learning, collaboration, and continuous
improvement over innate-talent framing.

## Business lines relevant to tech hiring

- **Microsoft Azure**: cloud infrastructure and platform services, Microsoft's largest growth
  engine and a major competitor to AWS and GCP.
- **Microsoft 365 & Teams**: productivity and collaboration software used by enterprises
  worldwide.
- **Developer Division**: GitHub, Visual Studio, TypeScript, and the broader developer-tools
  ecosystem.
- **Windows & Devices**: core OS engineering and Surface hardware integration.
- **Gaming (Xbox)**: game platform engineering and cloud gaming infrastructure.
- **AI**: deep investment across Copilot products and Azure AI services, increasingly central to
  hiring across nearly every division.

## Culture and hiring style

Microsoft's interviews assess core CS fundamentals and coding ability similarly to other big tech
firms, but with an explicit, named cultural pillar — **growth mindset** — that shows up directly
in behavioral questions: interviewers look for genuine curiosity, comfort with being wrong, and
collaborative rather than competitive framing of past experiences. System design is weighted more
heavily for senior roles (SDE II and above) than for new grads. Microsoft also runs a distinctive
**"As Appropriate" (AA) round** — a final interviewer, often a senior engineer or manager not on
the immediate hiring team, who makes the final go/no-go call after reviewing the full loop's
feedback.

## Typical roles hired for

Software Engineer (SDE, various levels), Program Manager (product-adjacent, non-pure-engineering),
Cloud Solution Architect, Data & Applied Scientist, and DevOps/Site Reliability roles across
Azure and the broader product portfolio.
