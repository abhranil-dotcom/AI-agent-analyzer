## Preparation Tips

- Practice Medium-to-Hard DSA problems, but expect a somewhat more collaborative, discussion-driven
  interview style than pure algorithm-grilling.
- For SDE II+ roles, practice system design with an Azure-flavored lens (many interviewers frame
  questions around real Azure services and their trade-offs).
- Prepare genuine "growth mindset" stories — a time you were wrong, a time you learned something
  under pressure — intellectual humility is explicitly valued over a flawless track record.
- Practice explaining both directions of giving and receiving technical feedback.
- Be ready to defend specific design decisions in your resume projects — Microsoft interviewers
  commonly spend real time on this.
- Stay calm under the "As Appropriate" round — it's framed as a holistic sanity check, not an
  adversarial gatekeeping round.
