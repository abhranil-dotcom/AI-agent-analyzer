## How Amazon Probes Your Resume

Amazon interviewers "Dive Deep" into resume projects — expect sustained, detailed follow-up
questioning on any project you list, well past a surface-level description. They're checking for
genuine ownership (did you actually do this, and do you understand every layer of it) and will
keep asking "why" until they either find the edge of your understanding or confirm real depth.

Common follow-up angles:
- "Why did you choose this specific technology/architecture over alternatives?"
- "What would break first if this had to handle 100x the load?"
- "What was the single hardest technical decision, and what did you consider before making it?"
- "What would you do differently if you rebuilt this today?"
- "Tell me about a specific bug or failure in this project and how you resolved it."

For AI/ML or modern backend projects (LangChain, FastAPI, Azure OpenAI, vector databases), expect
deep technical follow-ups on the actual mechanics, not just the tool names — Amazon interviewers
are comfortable pushing until they find where your understanding gets shallow.
