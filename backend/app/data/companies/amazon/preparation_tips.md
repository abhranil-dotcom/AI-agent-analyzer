## Preparation Tips

- Prepare 8-10 STAR-format stories from your real experience, each mappable to multiple Leadership
  Principles — you'll reuse them across the OA, phone screen, and every onsite round.
- Practice Medium-to-Hard DSA problems with a focus on precise Big-O analysis; interviewers expect
  you to state complexity unprompted.
- For SDE II+ roles, practice system design fundamentals (caching, load balancing, database
  sharding, consistency trade-offs).
- Never present a story where you had zero flaws or disagreements — genuine ownership and
  learning from failure matter more than a flawless narrative.
- Expect every coding round to include behavioral follow-ups — don't treat coding and behavioral
  prep as separate tracks.
- Research the specific Leadership Principles most relevant to the team/role you're interviewing
  for (e.g. "Customer Obsession" for customer-facing roles, "Dive Deep" for technical depth roles).
