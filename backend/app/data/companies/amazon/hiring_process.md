## Hiring Process

1. **Online Assessment (OA)** — for SDE roles, typically 2 coding problems (Medium difficulty,
   LeetCode-style) plus a work-style/behavioral simulation section aligned to the Leadership
   Principles.
2. **Phone/Video Screen** — one round with a coding problem (Medium) and Leadership-Principle
   behavioral questions in STAR format.
3. **Onsite Loop (4-5 rounds)** — a mix of:
   - **Coding rounds** (2-3) — Medium-to-Hard DSA problems, evaluated on correctness, complexity
     analysis, and clean code, always paired with behavioral follow-ups.
   - **System Design round** (for SDE II and above) — designing a scalable system (e.g. a URL
     shortener, a rate limiter, a notification service) with attention to trade-offs at scale.
   - **Bar Raiser round** — conducted by a trained interviewer from outside the immediate team,
     focused heavily on Leadership Principles and cross-checking consistency of the candidate's
     signal across the whole loop. The Bar Raiser has effective veto power on the hiring decision.
4. **Hiring Committee / Debrief** — all interviewers submit independent written feedback mapped to
   specific Leadership Principles before discussing as a group; the Bar Raiser ensures the bar
   isn't lowered due to team pressure to fill a role quickly.

## Timeline

Typically 3-6 weeks from OA to offer for SDE roles, sometimes longer depending on loop scheduling
and role level.

## What's assessed at each stage

The OA and phone screen filter for baseline coding ability; onsite coding rounds assess DSA depth
and code quality; system design assesses scalability thinking (for senior roles); every round
also scores Leadership Principles behavioral signal, and the Bar Raiser specifically checks for
consistency and long-term hiring-bar protection over short-term staffing pressure.
