## About Accenture

Accenture is a global professional services company providing technology consulting, digital
transformation, cloud, and outsourcing services across nearly every major industry. It positions
itself more as a consulting firm with deep technology delivery capability than a pure IT-services
vendor, and has one of the largest India-based delivery workforces of any global tech company.

## Business lines relevant to tech hiring

- **Technology consulting**: strategy and advisory work alongside implementation.
- **Cloud, data & AI**: a major growth area — cloud migration, data engineering, and applied
  generative AI solutions for enterprise clients.
- **SAP & enterprise platforms**: one of the largest global SAP implementation practices.
- **Digital engineering**: full-stack development, mobile, and API/microservices work.
- **Security**: a dedicated cybersecurity consulting and managed-services practice.

## Culture and hiring style

Accenture hires through campus drives, off-campus assessments, and lateral hiring across a very
wide range of seniority and specialization. Because much of its work is consulting-adjacent,
interviews weigh business/technology versatility and communication more than at pure delivery
shops — candidates are often expected to reason about a technology choice's business impact, not
just its technical correctness. The technical bar for lateral hires, especially in cloud/data/AI
and architecture roles, is moderate-to-high.

## Typical roles hired for

Associate Software Engineer (freshers), Application Development Analyst, Cloud Engineer, Data
Engineer, SAP Consultant, Security Analyst — plus senior lateral roles in solution architecture,
technology strategy, and engagement/program management.
