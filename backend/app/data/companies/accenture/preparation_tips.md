## Preparation Tips

- Practice explaining not just what you built, but why — Accenture interviewers consistently push
  on the business rationale behind technical decisions.
- Revise core fundamentals (OOP, REST API design, SQL vs NoSQL trade-offs) alongside system-design
  basics like caching and rate limiting.
- Familiarize yourself with Accenture's stated core values beforehand; values-alignment questions
  are asked directly, not just implied.
- Prepare a concrete example of balancing a technical solution against a business constraint (cost,
  time, client requirements).
- If applying to cloud/data/AI tracks, brush up on high-level trade-offs (e.g. data warehouse vs
  data lake, serverless vs always-on) rather than deep implementation trivia.
- Be ready to discuss comfort working across different industries or client domains over time.
