## How Accenture Probes Your Resume

Accenture interviewers treat resume projects as case studies — they want to understand not just
what was built, but the reasoning and trade-offs behind it, consistent with the firm's consulting
orientation. Expect the conversation to move from "what did you build" to "why did you build it
that way" fairly quickly, often with a "what would you do differently" follow-up.

Common follow-up angles:
- "What business or user problem was this project actually solving?"
- "Why this technology/architecture over the alternatives you considered?"
- "What constraints (time, cost, team size) shaped your decisions?"
- "How would this design need to change if the scale increased 10x?"

For projects using modern frameworks or AI tooling (e.g. LangChain, FastAPI, Azure OpenAI), expect
a question testing genuine conceptual understanding, not just familiarity with the name.
