## Hiring Process

## For freshers

1. **Cognitive & Technical Assessment** — aptitude (logical reasoning, numerical, verbal) plus a
   technology-specific section chosen at application time (e.g. programming, or a
   non-coding technology aptitude track for some roles).
2. **Technical Interview** — covers CS fundamentals, the primary language/stack on the resume,
   and academic/personal projects, with an emphasis on explaining trade-offs, not just definitions.
3. **HR Interview** — communication, career motivation, relocation flexibility, and a values-fit
   discussion (Accenture explicitly evaluates alignment with its stated core values in this
   round).

## For lateral/experienced hires

1. **Resume screening** against the specific role, account, and skill requirements.
2. **1-2 Technical Interviews** — deep dive into the relevant stack, system/solution design for
   more senior roles, and how past project decisions were made (not just what was built).
3. **HR/Managerial round** — compensation band, account/project fitment, and notice period.

## Timeline

Campus hiring typically takes 3-6 weeks from assessment to offer. Lateral hiring timelines vary
significantly by account urgency and role seniority — from under 2 weeks for high-priority roles
to over a month for specialized architecture positions.

## What's assessed at each stage

The cognitive/technical assessment filters for aptitude and baseline technical literacy; the
technical interview filters for depth and the ability to reason about trade-offs and business
impact (a recurring theme given Accenture's consulting orientation); the HR round filters for
values alignment, communication, and logistics fit.
