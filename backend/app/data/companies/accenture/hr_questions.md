## HR Questions

### Tell me about yourself.
Keep it tight and outcome-oriented — Accenture interviewers often respond better to answers that
connect your background to business impact, not just technical description.

### Why do you want to join Accenture?
Reference its position as a technology consulting leader (not just a delivery shop), the breadth
of industries you'd be exposed to, and specific growth areas like cloud, data & AI.

### What do you know about Accenture's core values?
Accenture explicitly screens for values alignment — familiarize yourself with stated values like
client value creation, one global network, respect for the individual, and integrity, and be
ready to connect one to a personal example.

### Describe a situation where you had to balance a technical solution with business constraints.
Central to Accenture's consulting-oriented interviews — use a real (even academic) example where
you had to consider cost, time, or client needs alongside the "ideal" technical answer.

### Are you comfortable working across different industries/domains over your career?
Accenture staffs people across many verticals — express genuine openness or explain any real
constraints clearly.

### What are your strengths and weaknesses?
Back the strength with a specific outcome; for weaknesses, be honest and describe a concrete
improvement step.

### Describe a time you had to persuade someone to adopt your point of view.
Reflects Accenture's consulting DNA — client and stakeholder influence skills matter here.

### Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
Frame around growing into more strategic/architectural responsibility within Accenture.

### Why should we hire you?
Connect specific skills to both technical delivery and the ability to communicate/consult, which
Accenture explicitly values.

### How do you handle working with ambiguous or evolving client requirements?
Common in consulting work — describe a real instance of adapting to shifting scope.

### Are you willing to relocate or travel for client engagements?
Some Accenture roles involve onsite client work — answer honestly about your flexibility.

### Do you have any questions for us?
Ask something specific — e.g. about a growth practice area (cloud/data/AI) or how staffing across
accounts typically works.
